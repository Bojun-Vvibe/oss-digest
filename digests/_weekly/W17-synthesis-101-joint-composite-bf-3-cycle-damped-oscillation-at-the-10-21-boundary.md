## Why H_damped-oscillation is the better framing

**(a) The amplitude trajectory is monotonic on the most recent transition.** The 5 boundary-crossing magnitudes are 0.418, 0.293, 0.417, 0.401, **0.180**. The first four are consistent with a noise floor around ~0.4 decade (range 0.293–0.418, stdev ~0.06). The fifth is **3× below the noise floor** — a structural break, not a noise excursion.

**(b) The boundary-oscillation framing predicts indefinite oscillation, but the cumulative inference state has converged on a fixed magnitude.** Each boundary-crossing event in the trajectory does not displace the long-run mean; the trajectory's **center of mass** is stable at ~×10²¹ across all 9 ticks. **(c) The cum decade-marker BF is converging in parallel.** Synth #100's cum decade-marker BF is at ×3.78 after three confirming instances; if the framework is converging toward a stationary regime, the joint composite BF should also converge. The two convergences are observed in parallel — consistent with a **regime-convergence event** at the joint composite BF level.
