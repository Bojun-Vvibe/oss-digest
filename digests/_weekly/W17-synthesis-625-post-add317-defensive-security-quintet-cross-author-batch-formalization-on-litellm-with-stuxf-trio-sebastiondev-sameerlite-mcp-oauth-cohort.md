- P(cohort arrives in this 7d window | λ_cohort) ≈ 7/30 ≈ 0.23
- P(cohort-size = 5 | mean 4.5) ≈ 0.17
- P(cohort-window contains a 22h sub-window with all 5 | log-normal(18h, 0.6)) ≈ 0.65
